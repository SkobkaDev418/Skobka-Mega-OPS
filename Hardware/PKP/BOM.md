Below is the complete list of parts required to assemble the Control Panel (CP) unit according to the current schematic.

### 1. Controllers & Modules
* **Arduino Mega 2560** ‚Äî 1 pc. (Main MCU).
* **RFID Module RC522** ‚Äî 1 pc. (Access card reader).
* **DFPlayer Mini** ‚Äî 1 pc. (Sound module).
* **5V Relay Module (1-ch)** ‚Äî 1 pc. (Load/Signage control).

### 2. Resistors
* **2 kOhm** ‚Äî 1 pc. (Protection resistor for DFPlayer RX line).
* **1 kOhm** ‚Äî 8 pcs. (For zone LED indicators Z1-Z8).
* **4.7 kOhm** ‚Äî 8 pcs. (End-of-Line resistors for loop monitoring).

### 3. Indicators & Peripherals
* **LEDs** ‚Äî 10 pcs. (8 for zones, 1 for arming status, 1 for power).
* **Buzzer** ‚Äî 1 pc. (Siren).
* **Speaker (8 Ohm)** ‚Äî 1 pc. (Voice notification).
