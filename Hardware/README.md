This folder contains the entire hardware part of the project: **Control Panel**, **sensors**, **modules**, and auxiliary equipment.

### What's inside:
* **Schematics & Photos:** Visual guides for assembly and connection of all system components.
* **Manufacturing Files (Gerber):** Everything needed to order and produce PCBs.
* **3D Models (STL):** Files for 3D printing the device enclosure and sensor mounts.
* **Bill of Materials (BOM):** A list of all required components, parts, and modules for procurement.

### Purpose:
This folder serves as a complete technical archive for recreating the device in hardware ‚Äî from ordering PCBs and printing the case to final assembly and soldering.
